  • Answer: Some believe this practice is linked to ancient Egypt, Indian Tantra, the Kabbalah or the I Ching. However, no scholarly research has unearthed any evidence to verify these associations.
  • Question: How many tarot cards should you choose for a reading?
  • Answer: Two spreads are recommended for novices: the Celtic Cross and the three-card pull. The former entails pulling ten cards, while the latter entails only three.
